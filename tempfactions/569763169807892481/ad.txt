In 1917, The Clark Company was credited with inventing the forklift, although the true creator’s name has been lost through time. This gasoline powered prototype device quickly caught on, and today, there are more than a million forklifts in use in warehouses across the world. 

As the Forklift Faction, we wish to be a peaceful group. The Forklift Faction is a trading/cargo group that doesn’t battle, but we are good in the engineering department and the more technical side of things, although perhaps that means we could use our capabilities for warfare…
